## The Idea

<!-- What problem did you see? What was the spark? -->

Meat processing workers face some of the highest rates of musculoskeletal injury in any industry. Kelly Starrett's Ready State methodology has proven results for movement quality and injury prevention, but it's locked behind expensive coaching and gym culture. MoveWell brings it to the factory floor.
