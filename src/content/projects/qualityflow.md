## The Idea

<!-- What problem did you see? What was the spark? -->

Food safety compliance in ANZ is dominated by tools built for auditors, not for the people on the floor actually doing the work. HACCP plans live in binders. Temperature logs are paper-based. The gap between what compliance requires and what operators experience daily is massive.

QualityFlow was born from watching this gap firsthand in meat processing and food manufacturing environments.
