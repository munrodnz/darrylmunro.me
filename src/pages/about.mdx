## What I Do

I've led architecture across complex enterprise estates — ERP, integration platforms, data, AI — and I've watched enough $15M initiatives fail to know that the technology is rarely the actual problem.

I work at the intersection of **enterprise architecture** and the humans who actually have to live inside the systems we build. Strong opinions, loosely held. I build things, write about what I find, and call out what doesn't work — including my own prior positions.

Currently building [QualityFlow](/), exploring knowledge graphs for compliance, and running **ThinkDifferent** — a workplace neurodiversity community.

## Neurodiversity

I'm openly AuDHD and an advocate for neurodiversity in the workplace. I'm a certified Good Yarns facilitator and work with [Beehyve Health](https://www.beehyve.health/) on workplace neurodiversity programmes.

I write regularly about what it's like to think differently in organisations that weren't designed for it.
